Philosophy

A studio built on the belief that technology should serve people — not the other way around.

A
Aprici Inc. · Est. 2026

Aprici is an independent software studio based in Ontario, Canada. We identify problems worth solving — in cognitive health, professional intelligence, and adjacent fields — and build the applications and platforms that solve them.

Every product begins with a real human need rather than a technology looking for a use case. We pair careful product thinking with modern AI to make software people actually want to use, and keep using.

We're small by design. That means we choose our work carefully, ship slowly, and treat every release as something we'd be proud to put our name on.

Consumer App · iOS

Zenelia

Daily brain health in 90 seconds.

An AI-powered cognitive training app that adapts to your mood, your sleep, and the patterns in your performance. Built on the science behind the ACTIVE Trial — the largest long-term study of cognitive training ever conducted.

  • Twelve adaptive brain training games
  • Personal AI coach for every session
  • Monthly health report for your doctor
  • Privacy-first by design
